Carolina Herrera

About Me

Hi!

I'm Carolina Herrera, a Dominican-born creative technologist from Miami, Florida. I've been drawn to making things for as long as I can remember, from doodling in the margins of notebooks to shooting videos with friends. That early curiosity has shaped how I approach my work today: as a chance to try new things and bring ideas to life in experimental ways.

With my Interactive Media Arts major and double minor in Game Design and Web Applications from NYU, my work blends animation, coding, video, and storytelling to create interactive and playful experiences. I enjoy exploring how different tools and mediums can work together to say something unexpected.

I've worked on everything from livestream productions to coding games and making video graphics. Each project has taught me something new and strengthened my passion for combining technology and creativity.

As I look ahead to post-grad life, I'm excited to keep experimenting, learning, and creating. I'm always looking for what's next.
